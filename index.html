Xiong Yuanjun's Homepage

Mr.Xiong Yuanjun - 熊元骏

Senior Applied Scientist at Amazon Rekognition


Seattle, Washington, U.S.

yjxiong at ie.cuhk.edu.hk

Mr. Xiong Yuanjun is currently a senior applied scientist at Amazon Rekognition. He was previously a postdoctoral fellow in department of information engineering, CUHK, working closely with Prof. Lin Dahua and Prof. Tang Xiaoou. Yuanjun received his Ph.D. from the department of information engineering at CUHK in 2016 and B.Eng from department of automation at Tsinghua University in 2012. Detailed CV is also provided here.
